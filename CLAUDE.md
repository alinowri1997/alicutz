# Business Rules (Permanent)

## Business Model

Ali Cutz is a premium luxury barber service focused on private appointments.

The main website goal is to encourage direct WhatsApp contact.

## Pricing Policy

Never display service prices anywhere on the website.

Do not create pricing tables.

Do not mention starting prices.

Do not mention estimated prices.

Do not include price ranges.

When pricing would normally appear, use a premium WhatsApp consultation CTA instead.

## Primary Conversion Goal

WhatsApp is always the primary action across all pages and components.

Preferred CTA language:

- WhatsApp Consultation
- Book via WhatsApp
- Message on WhatsApp

Do not prioritize contact forms over WhatsApp.

## Service Area Positioning

Primary positioning must emphasize premium barber appointments at the client location.

Target scenarios:

- Hotel guests
- Tourists
- Business travelers
- Expats
- Clients preferring service at home

Always communicate premium at-hotel and private-residence service availability in Istanbul.

## Salon Visit Rules

Some clients may visit the salon, but never publish the exact salon address.

Only mention the general area:

Osmanbey / Bomonti, Istanbul

If exact location is needed, direct users to WhatsApp.

## Google Maps Rules

Do not embed an exact Google Maps location.

Use an elegant location section that states:

- Salon is in Osmanbey / Bomonti area
- Exact location is shared after booking via WhatsApp

## Copywriting Style Rules

Tone must feel exclusive, premium, and private.

Position the experience as a private luxury barber service, not a typical barbershop.

## Conversion Strategy Rules

Every page must naturally guide users to one action:

Send a WhatsApp message to book an appointment or request exact salon location.

Apply these rules consistently to all current and future pages and components.
